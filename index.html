Bike Sharing in L.A

One way trip to the moon

Using both one way and round trips from our data, bikers drove nearly 250000 miles. Enough milage to travel the globe nearly 10 times. Enough milage to have 110 people bike the entirity of the Tour de France. Distance was calculated by seperating one - way trips and round trips. Distance in one - way trips was calculated using google's distance matrix API, accumlating a total of 130690.67 miles. Using an average biking speed of 9.6 mph (taken from average biking speed in Copenhagen) and the total duration of round trips, we get 9.6 * 11614 = 111492.959 miles.

800,000 dollars

Average gas prices in L.A during this time period were 3.29 (FRED data), this means that bikers saved nearly 800,000 dollars.

196,100 pounds of CO2 emmision

Buring one gallon of gas creates 20 pounds of carbon dioxide. According to the EPA, average MPG of a car in the U.S is 24.7 MPG. This means that bikers prevented over 196,100 pounds of CO2 emmision.
